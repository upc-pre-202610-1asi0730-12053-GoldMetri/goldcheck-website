FAQs

  • What is GUARDIANTS and how does it work? +

    GUARDIANTS is an intelligent vehicle monitoring platform that uses IoT sensors and telemetry to provide real-time tracking, security alerts, and fleet management from any device.

  • Is GUARDIANTS compatible with any type of vehicle? +

    Yes, GUARDIANTS is compatible with most vehicles manufactured after 2005 that have a standard OBD-II port, including cars, trucks, motorcycles, and heavy machinery.

  • How secure is my data on the platform? +

    We use end-to-end encryption and comply with international data protection regulations. Your data is stored in certified secure servers and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

  • Can I manage multiple vehicles at the same time? +

    Absolutely. Our enterprise plan allows you to manage unlimited vehicles from a single dashboard, with role-based access control for your team members.

  • What happens if my vehicle goes to an area without signal? +

    The device stores data locally and syncs automatically once connectivity is restored, ensuring no tracking gaps in your vehicle's history.
